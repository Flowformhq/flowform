# FlowForm License Promise

**Last updated:** May 2026

FlowFormHQ commits to the following:

1. The FlowForm engine (`flowform/`) will always be available under the terms of the GNU Affero General Public License, version 3 or any later version published by the Free Software Foundation.

2. We will never relicense the FlowForm engine under a more restrictive license. Specifically, we will never adopt the Business Source License (BSL), Server Side Public License (SSPL), Fair Source License (FSL), or any license that restricts production use, imposes field-of-use restrictions, or introduces time-delayed open-source gating.

3. Starter kits and SDKs will always be available under the MIT license.

4. This promise is irrevocable. It applies to all versions of the FlowForm engine, past and future.

5. A commercial license option will always be available for organizations that cannot accept AGPLv3 terms. The commercial license is additive — it does not remove or diminish the AGPLv3 option.

## Why this matters

The open-source community has seen several projects retroactively change their license to restrict usage (HashiCorp, Elastic, Redis Labs). This creates uncertainty for adopters who build on open-source foundations.

We believe the AGPLv3 + commercial dual-license model is sustainable without restricting the open-source offering. If it ever becomes unsustainable, we will seek alternative business models before considering license changes — and this promise binds us against restrictive relicensing regardless.

## Dual-license details

- **AGPLv3-or-later:** Use, modify, and self-host freely. Network use (providing FlowForm as a service to users) requires making your modifications available under AGPLv3. See the [full license text](https://www.gnu.org/licenses/agpl-3.0.en.html).

- **Commercial license:** For organizations that cannot accept AGPLv3 obligations (embedded use in proprietary SaaS, internal deployment in AGPL-restricted environments, or preference for indemnification). Contact licensing@flowformhq.com.

## Scope

This promise covers the contents of the `flowform/` repository — the headless form and workflow engine. It does not cover the hosted SaaS product at flowformhq.com, which is proprietary and separate.
